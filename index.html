What is EMEVINEFINANCE?

EmevineFinance is a project built on multi-blockchains, where trade is performed. EmevineFinance provides multiple trading options ranging from decentralized cryptocurrency trading, liquidity mining, staking, NFTs marketplace and Multi-vendor Ecommerce.

ALL-IN-ONE PLATFORM

Finding decentralized exchanges on different blockchains can be overwhelming and tiring.

EmevineFinance provides various decentralized exchange on different blockchains on a single platform.



Passive income

EmevineFinance seeks to offer an adequate means of earning passive income for its users. EmevineFinance will provide VinePools and Farms, where users can provide liquidity, stake and farm to earn rewards. Relax and let your Vines do the job!!!

NFT Marketplace

We love Artworks!
NFTS are more than a passing craze. EmevineFinance provides a conducive NFT Marketplace where Artists can exhibit their digital artworks, collectibles, music, videos, digital lands and lots more. Buy/Sell or launch your own NFT store in minutes. Physical exhibitions will also be held periodically in different parts of the world.

Ecommerce

Buy and sell anywhere in the world. EmevineFinance provides an Ecommerce platform for multi-vendors to showcase their goods and sell. Set up your store in minutes with various options.
